ABOUT US

Raising a Generation Rooted in Purpose

Nurtured Vine Ministries is a Christ-centered developmental organization focused on raising spiritually grounded and intellectually equipped young people aged 0–25.

We also equip parents, mentors, and caregivers with the tools and wisdom needed to nurture the next generation into their God-given identities and callings.

Our Vision

To see a generation of young people who are deeply rooted in their identity in Christ, walking in purpose, and transforming their world through faith, knowledge, and service.

Our Mission

To nurture and develop young people spiritually, intellectually, and socially through Christ-centered programs, mentorship, and community, while equipping parents and caregivers to raise the next generation with intentionality and purpose.

OUR COMMUNITIES

Growing Together in Faith

Two vibrant communities united by one mission — nurturing identities and transforming generations.


Light 25 Community

A vibrant community of young people aged 0–25, united in faith and purpose. Through mentorship, fellowship, and discipleship, we help young people discover and walk in their God-given identity.

Nurturers Community

A supportive network for parents, mentors, and caregivers dedicated to raising the next generation. We provide resources, training, and community to equip nurturers for the journey.
