About Us

Our Ministry Identity

Nurtured Vine Ministries is a Christ-centered developmental organization focused on raising spiritually grounded and intellectually equipped young people (0–25), while also equipping parents and mentors for the sacred task of nurturing the next generation.

Vision Statement

To see a generation of young people who are deeply rooted in their identity in Christ, walking in purpose, and transforming their world through faith, knowledge, and service.

Mission Statement

To nurture and develop young people spiritually, intellectually, and socially through Christ-centered programs, mentorship, and community — while equipping parents and caregivers.

Core Values

What Guides Us

The principles that shape everything we do and every life we touch.

Intimacy with God

We prioritize a deep, personal relationship with God as the foundation of all growth and transformation.

Integrated Intelligence

We believe in developing the whole person — spiritually, intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

Authenticity

We encourage genuine expression of faith and identity, creating safe spaces for honest growth.

Stewardship

We cultivate responsible management of the gifts, talents, and resources God has entrusted to us.

Care and Support

We foster a nurturing environment where every individual is valued, supported, and empowered to thrive.

Our Objectives

What We Aim to Achieve

Equip young people with spiritual foundations and biblical knowledge

Develop leadership, critical thinking, and creative skills

Build a community of nurturers committed to intentional parenting and mentorship

Create transformative programs and experiences that shape character

Raise a generation that impacts their communities and the world for Christ

Nurturing Identities, Transforming Generations. Rooted in Christ, Transforming the World.
